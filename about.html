  • Introductory article to the exhibition catalog

    Posted by: Super User

    Almas Nurggetaev is one of the most creatively active young artists in the modern fine art of Kazakhstan. This could be convinced of the exhibition, which was held at the State Museum of Arts of the Republic of Kazakhstan. A. Kasteev in August 2019. The large-format works were performed in the new style for the artist. The author is not afraid to experiment with the shape, color, easily writes bold perspectives. By minimizing the details, it enhances the emotional sound of paintings, thanks to the silhouette, almost a monochrome color decision. The ability to build a composition, pass the movement, the internal voltage in the picture is distinctive features of the creative method of the artist.

  • China, Kazakhstan and Russia in the life and work of Kazakhstan painting Kamil Mullashev

    Posted by: Mukazhanov Kulzhazir Zhumadilievna

    Today, as at the turn of the twentieth century, the XX-XXI centuries, there are many artists, fate and careers of which could be the subject of a special scientific research. The choice of painter Kamil Valihahamedovich Mullashev was non-random. On his life and creative path, the artist overcame a lot of obstacles, but his talent, performance, dedication and faith in the correctness of life priorities allowed him to achieve a high professional level and recognition of the audience and colleagues-artists. Currently, Mullashev - Honored Artist of the Republic of Kazakhstan and People's Artist of Tatarstan, Laureate of State Prizes of Kazakhstan and Tatarstan, Honorary Academician of the Russian Academy of Arts, Honorary Professor of Chinese Universities - Pedagogical Institute Khan-Shan City of Chao Jao and University Shan-Tao, Professor State University of Arts Astana.
